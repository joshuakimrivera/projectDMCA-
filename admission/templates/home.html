Who are We?

DMCA is a non-stock, non-profit, non-secretarian learning institution for children, where every child is accepted and given the oppurtunity to quality educatio regardless of race, naionality, religion and socio-economic status.
DMCA is a Methodist Church-related school, a mission arm of nature. It aims to develop the child's potentials to the maximum through an integrated and coordinated plan of work and play activities suited to te child's age, interests, talents and abilities.
DMCA offers conducive classrooms, educational devices and materials especially prepared for the intellectual, emotional, physical, social and spiritual development of the children.
DMCA is recognized by the government through the Department of Education.







We Strive to provide young children experiences in Christian Living

through balance program of development, and to simulate them to develop their optimum potential.
